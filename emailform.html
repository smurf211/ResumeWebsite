HTML Forms and Generating Emails

Any good website includes a contact page, and this one is no exception. Check out my contact me page here. You can also view the same form at the bottom of this page, and use it to generate an email based on your input. The email will be sent to whoever you like. To understand how to do this we need to build a form, and then select the users inputs to build an email that is formatted correctly.
